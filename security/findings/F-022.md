**Description:**
`computeSigningHash()` doesn't do `.drop(2)` for sender/nonce, although `computeUserOpHash()` does. Different hashes for same data.

**Resolution:**
STALE — `computeSigningHash()` was never present in the codebase. The external audit (inbox-external-cloud-2026-06-25.md) analyzed a different version/branch. EIP-712 typehashes (`QUOTE_TYPEHASH`, `EIP712_DOMAIN_TYPEHASH`) are correctly defined at PaymasterService.kt:32-39 with `Hash.sha3String()`. `computeUserOpHash()` at line 329 consistently uses `.drop(2)`.
